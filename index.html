Tejomay is a data scientist, researcher, and a graduate student at NYU, where he is affiliated with the Center for Social Media and Participation. His previous work at such institutions as MDRC, The University of Chicago, and Columbia University has ranged from building pipelines for large-scale environmental studies to winning a machine learning competition at Princeton.

Tejomay's approach to data science not only addresses the problems at hand, but also those that lay hidden down the road — by combining theoretical rigour with meticulous design, he develops systems that, in accordance with the central task of data science, genuinely inhabit their area of function (cf. "What is Data Science?" above).

Tejomay enjoys reading and writing — though typically not about himself — and can be reached tejomay@nyu.edu. He looks forward to your correspondence.
